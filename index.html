About

Prayagraj EPark Gateway is a transformative digital initiative designed to bring the beauty, history, and vibrancy of Prayagraj's two most iconic green spaces — Hathi Park and Company Garden — into the digital age. Moreover, the website will serve as a real-time guide, offering up-to-date information on events, activities, and amenities within the parks
